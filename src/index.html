Computer Science Graduate Entrepreneurs

The CSGE (Computer Science Graduate Entrepreneurs) is a student organization for graduate students and alumni of the Computer Science department at UC Berkeley. Our vision is a thriving culture of entrepreneurship in the department that generates the world's most influential startups and entrepreneurs. We work toward this goal by collecting ideas, experience, and connections that transfer from generation to generation of entrepreneurial Berkeley CS graduate students and alumni.

If you are a Graduate student or Alumni of Berkeley CS or EE, and interested in entrepreneurship, please:

  • Join the CSGE grads mailing list csge@lists.eecs.berkeley.edu.
  • Come hang out (see calendar below). Join the slack channel #ge-general.
  • Become a club officer or advisor and shape the culture of the department. Email csge-officers@lists.eecs.berkeley.edu to learn more.
  • Check out or add to the CSGE wiki.
